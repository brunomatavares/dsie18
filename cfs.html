Call for Sponsors

You are invited to support the Doctoral Symposium in Informatics Engineering 2018 (DSIE'18), organized by the Doctoral Program in Informatics Engineering (ProDEI) at Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto (FEUP).

This is an ideal way to demonstrate your organization's commitment to the academic world, the corporate support is typically used to allow a greater number of students to attend DSIE’18. Your support is very important to the symposium, and the organization committee ensures that these contributions are well recognized.

If you are interested in sponsor this event please contact us at dsie2018@fe.up.pt
