Call for Papers

PhD and MsC students working in any area of Informatics Engineering are invited to present their scientific works to discuss their ideas and obtain a relevant feedback and profitable expert opinions from peers, professors and experienced researchers. Authors should submit a paper in English, carefully checked for correct grammar and spelling, addressing one of the conference topics. Each paper should clearly indicate the nature of its technical/scientific contribution, and the problems, domains or environments in which it is applicable. Papers must be formatted according to the Springer LNCS Template , with a length between 10 and 12 pages (including figures and references) and should report on substantial research supported by measurable results.

All proposed papers must be submitted electronically (in PDF format) using the Paper Submission Page (EasyChair system). At least one author for each accepted paper must register to ensure that the paper will be included in the conference proceeding. We would recommend that you respect the page limit and do not submit more than one paper per event. All papers will be single-blind reviewed by the scientific committee members with the aim to provide detailed and constructive feedback. All reviewers recommendations must be addressed by the authors before they submit the final version. Scientific contributions will be evaluated in terms of the following criteria:

  • Relevance
  • Originality
  • Quality (including technical content and accuracy)
  • Significance of the work
  • Presentation (organization, style and clearness)
  • Appropriateness of references to papers presented in earlier editions of the event
Authors of accepted papers will have 15 minutes to present their scientific contribution during the Presentation Sessions, followed by approximately 5 minutes of discussion.
